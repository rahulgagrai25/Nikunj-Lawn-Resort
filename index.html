About Nikunj's Byara

Jaipur's most exquisite destination for luxury weddings and events

Our Legacy

Nestled in the vibrant city of Jaipur, Nikunj's Byara Lawns and Resort is a testament to Rajasthan's royal heritage and modern luxury. We specialize in creating unforgettable wedding experiences and grand celebrations.

Our property features sprawling manicured lawns, elegant banquet halls, and luxurious accommodations, all designed to provide the perfect backdrop for your special occasions. With attention to every detail and a commitment to excellence, we ensure your event is nothing short of extraordinary.

From intimate gatherings to grand weddings, our team of experienced event planners and hospitality professionals will work with you to bring your vision to life, creating memories that will be cherished forever.
